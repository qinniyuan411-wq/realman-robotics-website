FAQs

What products and solutions does RealMan offer? south_east

RealMan offers a broad portfolio of products, including joint modules, robotic arms, complete robot systems, end effectors, and ecosystem accessories. We also provide system- and platform-level solution support for automation and embodied intelligence applications. Our current product lineup covers the RM, RML, ECO, and GEN series robotic arms, embodied dual-arm systems, RealBOT robots, as well as supporting joint products and development capabilities.

Which development environments and communication protocols does RealMan support? south_east

RealMan supports multiple communication protocols, including JSON and Modbus, and provides SDKs in C, C++, C#, Python, and other languages. Our products are also compatible with ROS and ROS 2, making it easier for customers to integrate RealMan solutions into a wide range of software systems and robotics development workflows.

Does RealMan provide SDKs, demo examples, and downloadable development resources? south_east

Yes. RealMan provides quick-start materials, API documentation, ROS / ROS 2 packages, sample demos, and a range of downloadable development resources, including secondary development kits, product catalogs, 3D models, and dimensional drawings. These materials help customers evaluate products, accelerate system integration, and streamline application development.

Does RealMan support secondary development and customized integration? south_east

Yes. RealMan provides open APIs, multi-language SDKs, and a well-developed technical documentation system to support secondary development and customized integration. Our resources also cover robotic arms, joint modules, AI capabilities, and data training, helping customers build and deploy their own application solutions more efficiently.
