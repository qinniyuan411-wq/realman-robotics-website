01

Robotic Arm ODM

Built on our proprietary joint modules, motion control stack, mass-production know-how, and extensive user feedback, we provide end-to-end custom robotic arm solutions.

We develop robotic arms with flexible 2- to 8-axis configurations, multiple DoF options, and customizable reach and payload specifications. Designed for high-precision applications across industrial, research, and human-robot collaboration scenarios, each solution is tailored to customer needs and delivered with rapid turnaround.

Complete Robot ODM

Modular robot development solutions designed for rapid customization and scalable deployment across customer-specific scenarios.

Designed around four core modules—chassis, legs, torso, and arms—our architecture uses standardized interfaces to speed integration while balancing performance, stability, and manufacturing efficiency.

Chassis Multi-terrain mobility for diverse deployment scenarios
Torso Centralized computing, perception, and interaction hub
Arms Proprietary joint-driven, multi-DoF manipulation system
Camera GMSL vision with ultra-low latency and industrial-grade stability
End-Effectors Compatible with leading grippers and dexterous hands
Result

Faster adaptation to custom requirements, shorter delivery cycles, and lower customization and maintenance costs.
